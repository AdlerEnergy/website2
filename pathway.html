Anaheim High Schools CTE Pathways


What are CTE Pathways?

Into: Anaheim High School offers a diverse range of 11 career and technology education pathways,with options that can fit your personal interests.

These pathways include exclusive options such as Biotechnology, as well as fun and engaging tracks in software development, system development, and entrepreneurship. Click on the links on the pathway you are interested to learn more.

What is CTE: CTE stands for Career and Technical education. CTE classes focus on the skills and knowledge needed to prepare for a specific careers. In the CTE classes you will have hands-on-training and real world experiences to gain practice skills and knowledge.

What are the benefits: CTE classes has valuable benefits like providing students with practical skills, career exploration opportunities, and preparing them for the workforce. These hands-on-programs allow students to explore a variety of career pathways and lets you figure out which one is right for you.
