A little about me...

My name is Aiden L. Brightbill and I was born in the picturesque coastal town of Laguna Beach, California. My diverse family background shaped my upbringing, with my mother hailing from Australia and my father being a lifelong resident of Southern California. My upbringing was a unique blend of cultures and experiences.

From a young age, I developed a passion for the world of technology. With dreams of making a mark in the computer science industry, I embarked on a journey to acquire the knowledge and skills required to achieve his aspirations.

My interests extended beyond the digital realm. I found joy and fulfillment in two distinct hobbies - programming, which allowed me to create and innovate, and collecting trading cards, which appealed to my love of detail and organization.

My story is a testament to the fusion of technology and diverse cultural influences, both of which have played a crucial role in shaping the person I am today.
