Software Systems and Development Pathway

Introduction to the Class

Welcome to the Computer Sci class! In this class, you'll gain a comprehensive understanding of software development, coding languages, and the exciting world of computer science. Our primary objectives include problem-solving skills, logical thinking, and creativity in software development.

Class Topics

Explore a large range of topics in this class, covering fundamental concepts of computer science. From programming languages like Java and Python to understanding algorithms and data structures, our curriculum ensures a well-rounded education in software systems and development.

Intro to Computer Science

Engage in hands-on projects and practical activities that bring computer science concepts to life. Our projects include creating interactive websites, developing simple applications, and collaborating on coding challenges. Explore your creativity while honing your programming skills!

AP Computer Science

Take your computer science knowledge to the next level with our AP Computer Science course. This advanced placement class not only delves deeper into complex topics but also provides an opportunity to take the AP test at the end of the year. Successfully passing the AP test may grant you college credit, allowing you to skip introductory college-level computer science courses.
